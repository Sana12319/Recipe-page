How to make a Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • Oreos
  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Flavoring
  • double cream
  • WhippedCream

Instructions

  1. Step 1:

    Crush 22 oreos in a ziplock bag. Reserve a few oreo crumbs to use later:

    Warning: Make sure to tightly close your ziplock bag because I once had an oreo confetti in my kitchen.

  2. Step 2:

    Mix the oreos from the ziplock bag with 60 grams of melted butter.

  3. Step 3:

    Place the oreo butter mix in a tray and then set aside:

  4. Step 4:

    In another bowl, mix 400 grams cream cheese, one tablespoon sugar, and some vanilla flavoring and wisk for one minute.:

  5. Step 5:

    Mix 400 ml double cream and wisk until stiff peaks form, then mix the reserved oreos:

  6. Step 6:

    Put the cream oreo mix on top of the butter mix and freeze for 30 minutes.

  7. Step 7:

    After freezing for 30 minutes, put whipped cream on the sides of the cake and decorate with oreos.

Final result

Put in the fridge and serve cold.
